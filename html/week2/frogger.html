THIS GAME IS BEST PLAYED ON A LAPTOP

use "wasd" keys to move the ball to the beige top to score a point

w = up, a = left, s = down, d = right

don't hit the rectangles, or else you will lose a life and the ball size will decrease

scoring three points wins the game, you will lose if you run out of lives or if the ball gets too small
